About us

Cat shelter is a small shelter in Greenway. It was set up in 2010 by vet Jo Stevens after she found a starving, abandoned cat. She took the cat into her practice for treatment and he made a full recovery. After that she decided to set up the cat shelter to save other cats in a similar situation. Since then the shelter has grown and now has 6 paid staff and several volunteers.
